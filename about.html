About

History

Our philosophy is about nurturing talent and love for the Performing Arts. We strive to create excellence in technique, self discipline and self awareness. Hype Performing Arts Academy believes that dance provides an opportunity to develop rhythm, strength, co-ordination, flexibility, self esteem and confidence. Performance as a form of communication can inspire, express and engage mind and body.

We seek to provide excellence in the balance between a disciplined but fun and creative learning environment for the pre-schooler through to the adult; and the beginner to the advanced student.

Hype Performing Arts Academy promotes a family atmosphere and encourages our children to grow and develop bonds of lifelong friendships. We inspire our students to express their individuality and creativity while learning to be their very best!

Staff

Miss Jessica Wong

Miss Jess originally trained at the Jane Stanley Dance Centre on the Northern Beaches, and as an adult participated in many master classes both in Sydney and abroad. These classes have included Ballet, Jazz, Contemporary, Tap, Lyrical, Hip-Hop, Breakdancing and Street-Dancing. Miss Jess has indulged her passion and love of dancing and the performing arts for many years, and has successfully taught many classes in a range of dance styles, and choreographed professionally both in Sydney and abroad.

She has implemented and run several dance programs within many physical education pecialists and schools as well as producing and choreographing many productions.

Miss Jess is a talented and professional teacher who aims to pass her knowledge and talent to her students while providing a balance between a disciplined but fun and creative learning environment through the art form of dance.
